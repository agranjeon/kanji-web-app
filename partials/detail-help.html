Quick Reference

Please see our User Guide for detailed explanations of all of Kanji alive's features or watch our demonstration videos. If you are having technical difficulties, please refer to our Troubleshooting page.

詳細は、メーニューの「日本語」内にある使用説明とトラブルシューティングをご参照ください。

Navigation

Next/Previous kanji Use the left arrow and right arrow keys or click on the left and right single pointed brackets in the top navigation bar
Toggle kanji animation/typeface Use the up arrow or down arrow keys or click on the large image of the kanji.
Next/Previous typeface Use the SHIFT + left arrow and SHIFT + right arrow keys or click on the left and right single pointed brackets next to the name of the typeface
Return to Search Click on the text of the current search term on the far left of the navigation bar

Note: The order in which kanji appear in the detail view when selecting the next or previous kanji is dependant on the sort mode previously chosen in the search view.

Other Interactive Elements

Play/Pause kanji animation Use the spacebar or click on the play/pause icon in the center of the kanji animation controls or.
Next/Previous stroke Use the SHIFT + left arrow or SHIFT + right arrow keys, click the previous and next stroke icons on each side of the play/pause icon or move your mouse cursor over the stroke breakdown images.
Radical animation Click on the image on the far right of the radical field to view an animation illustrating the radical's historical derivation.
Examples Move your mouse cursor over a Japanese word in the Examples field to reveal its English meaning. Click on the play icon to the right of each example to hear its pronunciation.
Example sentences Click on the phrase to view the online dictionary entry for the target kanji in Kenkyusha's Luminous Dictionary.
